# Master Services Agreement (MSA) — TEMPLATE

> **TEMPLATE — NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** This document is a working template drafted for the DocQuery
> product. **A qualified lawyer must review and adapt it before it is used with any customer.** We do
> not self-certify it. It is provided so the product's commitments are written down and so the
> engineering can be built to *honor* them — not as a finished contract.

**Parties.** "Provider" = the operator of DocQuery. "Customer" = the law firm (or other organization)
that subscribes to the service. "Customer Data" = all documents, conversations, queries, generated
answers, and metadata the Customer or its users submit to or generate within the service.

## 1. The service
Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable right to access and use DocQuery (a
retrieval-augmented document analysis and review service) for Customer's internal business and legal
work, subject to this Agreement.

## 2. Data ownership (load-bearing)
**Customer retains all right, title, and interest in and to Customer Data.** Provider obtains no
ownership of Customer Data. Provider's rights are limited to those strictly necessary to provide the
service to Customer (storing, indexing, retrieving, and processing Customer Data on Customer's
instructions). Provider does **not** acquire any right to use Customer Data for its own purposes.

This ownership term is operationalized by the Data Processing Agreement ([DPA.md](DPA.md)), which
governs how Customer Data is processed, secured, disclosed to subprocessors, and erased.

## 3. Acceptable use & supervision
DocQuery is a tool that assists qualified professionals; it does not replace professional judgment.
Customer is responsible for the supervision of work product (the review-chain / approval model the
product provides), and for verifying AI-assisted output before relying on or releasing it. See the
[AI-use addendum](AI_USE_ADDENDUM.md).

## 4. Warranties & disclaimers
Provider will provide the service with reasonable skill and care. **AI-assisted output may be
incomplete or incorrect; it is provided without warranty of legal accuracy.** The product's
abstain/verify posture and human review chain exist precisely because the output must be checked.
Nothing in the service constitutes legal advice from Provider.

## 5. Confidentiality
Each party will protect the other's confidential information. Provider treats all Customer Data as
confidential and binds its personnel and subprocessors to confidentiality (the "agent of the attorney"
duty, contractualized in the [DPA](DPA.md) §2).

## 6. Liability
Liability is limited as set out in the executed order form / commercial terms. *A real lawyer sets
these caps; this template does not fix a number.*

## 7. Term, termination & offboarding
On termination, Customer may export Customer Data, and Provider will erase Customer Data per the
[DPA](DPA.md) §4 (erasure-on-termination), preserving only the immutable records of processing the law
requires. The product's export and erase capabilities make this real, not aspirational.

## 8. Order of precedence
The DPA governs data processing and prevails over this MSA on any data-processing conflict.
